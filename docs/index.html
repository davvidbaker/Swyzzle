  • Swyzzle is a desktop app that melts your screen (and possibly your GPU). Original inspiration came from IOGraphica and then I was all like, what if I could make my desktop melt?? o.O There is no purpose to Swizzle—besides pure entertainment.

    Swyzzle is like a screensaver in that it kicks in after letting your computer idle for some time. You can open the preferences with the shortcut ⌘, where you can then configure the timeout and customize the swyzzling mode.
